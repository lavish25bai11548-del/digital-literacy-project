# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP, passwords, or banking details with anyone
- Always verify the sender before clicking on any links
- Check website URLs carefully before entering login details
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Do not approve unknown UPI payment requests
- Regularly update your apps and devices for security
- Be cautious of messages that create urgency or panic
