# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## ✅ Do’s
- Use respectful and professional language
- Think before posting anything online
- Protect your personal information and privacy
- Verify information before sharing
- Maintain a positive digital presence

## ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share passwords or OTPs
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not spread fake news or misinformation
- Avoid oversharing personal details
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or online conflicts
